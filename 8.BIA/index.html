Introduction

The Building Industry Association of Okaloosa-Walton Counties is a professional organization of builders, developers and associates affiliated with the Florida Home Builders Association and the National Association of Home Builders.The BIA serves the construction industry and the communities and government agencies in the area by:

  • Providing information, education and resources;
  • Providing leadership to unify the industry;
  • Serving as the voice of the construction industry;
  • Promoting home ownership; and
  • Serving as the link for members between FHBA and NAHB.

The BIA will continually strive to increase membership so as to remain the source of construction industry information, and an effective service provider to its members.

Welcome to Coastal Village Homes...

Upper Cape Cod and Southeastern Massachusett’s online home for waterfront, retirement, vacation and relocation home real estate. Coastal Village Real Estate is a dedicated real estate site specializing in the marketing, sales and assisted buying of homes and real estate that are unique to coastal Massachusetts.

Whether it is a private oceanfront estate, a beachfront bungalow, an un-bridged or road accessible island property, or the ever- charming Cape Cod cottage, Coastal Village offers properties rich in scenic and aesthetic value.

From the breathtaking oceanfront homes of Marion, Mattapoisett and Bourne to the quaint, historic seaside village homes of Onset, Buzzard’s Bay and Sagamore Beach, Coastal Village provides every type of property seaside living affords, while working diligently to assure buyers unequaled selection and sellers successful representation.
